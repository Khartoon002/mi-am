What is Miamor?

Miamor is a next-generation digital platform where users can play games, publish articles, share content, and grow their social influence — all while earning real rewards like cash, data, and ownership shares.

How do I start earning on Miamor?

You can earn on Miamor by playing supported games, writing articles, completing social streak challenges, referring others, and participating in creator programs like TikTok monetization.

Is there a joining bonus?

Yes! All new users get 5GB of free data on their preferred network as a welcome gift. This helps you get started without spending a dime.

How does 'Play & Earn' work?

Simply log in daily and play games listed on the platform. You'll earn cash rewards — up to $20 per day — based on your activity and performance.

Can I earn money by writing articles?

Yes. Every article you publish earns you money for every view it receives. The more engagement your content generates, the more you earn.

What is the 25-Day Streak Challenge?

This is a social media challenge where you post Miamor-themed videos daily for 25 days. Completing the challenge earns you up to $50 and increases your visibility and followers.

How do I earn from referrals or team activity?

Miamor has a dual-direction referral reward system. You earn passively from both your uplines' (superior's) success and your downline team's activities, creating a circular income loop.

What are Miamor Shares?

As an active user, you receive fractional shares of the Miamor company. These shares grow in value over time and can be sold whenever you choose.

How do I boost my content visibility?

Use the Miamor Social Boast tool to amplify your content reach. It helps you attract more views, clicks, and followers organically or through challenges.

What is the Miamor Reward Blueprint?

It's our structured system of daily, weekly, and milestone rewards — based on actions like writing, sharing, playing, inviting, and more. You earn tokens, data, badges, and cash.

Is Miamor available in all countries?

Currently, Miamor is optimized for users in Nigeria and select regions. However, we're expanding rapidly, and international users can join and benefit where supported.

How do I withdraw my earnings?

Withdrawals can be made via bank transfer, airtime top-up, or other supported payout options. Just head to your wallet and request a payout when your balance meets the minimum threshold.

Is there a mobile app for Miamor?

Yes, the Miamor app will be available for Android and iOS. You can also access the platform from any browser on mobile or desktop.

Can I sell my Miamor shares?

Yes. You can sell your fractional shares on our platform-based marketplace or through periodic buyback programs, depending on current policies.
